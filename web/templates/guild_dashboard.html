Available Moderation Commands

  • /kick - Kick a member
  • /ban - Ban a member
  • /unban - Unban a user
  • /timeout - Timeout a member
  • /warn - Warn a member
  • /warnings - View member warnings
  • /clearwarnings - Clear member warnings
  • /purge - Delete multiple messages
  • /slowmode - Set channel slowmode
